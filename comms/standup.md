# Standup update template

> Audience: your immediate team, in the daily standup channel.
> Tone: short. Three lines, ideally. Nobody reads paragraph standups.

---

✅ **Shipped:** {{name}} — {{oneLiner}}
🔜 **Next:** {{What follows, or "nothing — done"}}
🚧 **Blockers:** {{None / one line if any}}

---

**Tone notes (delete on generation):**
- First-person, casual. "Shipped X" not "X has been delivered".
- No status emoji ladder beyond ✅ 🔜 🚧.
- If you can't say it in one line per row, the work isn't done yet.
