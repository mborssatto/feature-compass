# Launch communication template

> Audience: end users / customers, in-app or email.
> Tone: friendly, benefit-led, ends with a clear call-to-action. Short.

---

### {{name}} is here.

{{One or two sentences on what the user can now do — phrased as a benefit,
not a feature. "Never lose work to a flaky connection" beats "we added retry
logic".}}

{{Optional one-liner on how to try it. Direct, no hedging.}}

**{{Call to action}}** → {{link or in-app action}}

---

**Tone notes (delete on generation):**
- Talk to one person, not "users". "You can now..." not "users can now...".
- No "We're excited to announce...". Just say what's new.
- One emoji max if any. Subtle.
- Read it out loud — if you sound like a brochure, rewrite it.
